Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Contentify ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and Figma plugin.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate this service:

  • Contact form submissions — if you submit a category request or contact us, we collect your name, email address, and message content.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as page views and browser type, collected anonymously through Vercel Analytics (if enabled). No personally identifiable information is stored.

We do not collect account information, payment details, or track individual users across sessions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to category requests or support inquiries you send us.
  • To understand general usage patterns and improve the product.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Generated Data

All data generated by Contentify — including names, addresses, ID numbers, phone numbers, photos, and other personal details — is entirely synthetic and fictitious. It does not represent any real person, living or dead. We do not store any data generated during your session.

4. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We may use essential cookies required for basic site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — website hosting. Vercel may collect standard server log data (IP address, request time) in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
  • Figma — our plugin runs within the Figma platform and is subject to Figma's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — used to serve the Geist typeface. Google may log font requests per their Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. We do not maintain long-term databases of user-submitted information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at stylishfrancis@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Contentify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
